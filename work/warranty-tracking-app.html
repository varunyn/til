Warranty Tracking App

A product warranty tracker that stores receipts and manuals, then sends reminders before coverage expires.

The Problem

Warranty details usually end up scattered across email, paper receipts, manuals, and memory. By the time a product breaks, it is hard to know whether the warranty is still active or where the proof of purchase lives.

What Shipped

I built a warranty tracking app where users can save product details, upload receipts and manuals, and keep the important warranty information in one place. The app also sends reminders before a warranty expires, so there is time to file a claim, extend coverage, or plan a replacement.

Why It Matters

The useful part is reducing the small bits of admin that people tend to postpone. A single place for receipts, manuals, dates, and reminders makes warranty coverage easier to use when it actually matters.
