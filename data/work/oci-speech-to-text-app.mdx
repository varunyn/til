## The Problem

Speech-to-text services are easy to demo in isolation, but teams need to see the full application flow: upload a file, send it for processing, store the result, and show the transcription back in the UI.

## What Shipped

I built a Visual Builder application backed by OCI Functions and OCI AI Speech. The app demonstrates the full path from file upload through transcription, including the cloud services needed to make the workflow repeatable.

## Why It Matters

This is the kind of demo that helps people move from service awareness to implementation. It shows the service boundary, the UI interaction, and the operational pieces that make the feature usable.
